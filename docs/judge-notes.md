# Why AERIS Stands Out

Instead of only displaying AQI, AERIS enables governments to:

- Predict pollution
- Identify probable causes
- Simulate interventions
- Prioritize actions
- Measure expected impact

This transforms environmental monitoring into AI-assisted decision-making.

# Demo Tips

During the presentation:

- Focus on the problem before the technology.
- Explain why current platforms are insufficient.
- Demonstrate one complete decision workflow.
- Highlight Explainable AI and confidence scores.
- Keep AI at the center of every feature.

---

# Winning Checklist

- Clear Problem Statement
- Strong Research Validation
- Practical AI Usage
- Clean UI & UX
- End-to-End Workflow
- Explainable Predictions
- Real Business Value
- Government Impact

# Final Goal

The objective is not to build another AQI dashboard.

The objective is to demonstrate how AI can improve environmental decision-making through intelligent recommendations, policy simulation, and explainable insights.
