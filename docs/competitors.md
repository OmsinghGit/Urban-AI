# Project AERIS

# Competitor Analysis

## Objective

Analyze existing air quality platforms to identify industry best practices, product gaps, and opportunities for building an AI-powered Urban Decision Intelligence Platform.

---

# Executive Summary

Current air quality platforms excel at monitoring, visualization, and forecasting but provide very limited decision support.

Most systems answer:

> **"What is happening?"**

Very few answer:

- Why is pollution increasing?
- Which source contributes the most?
- What action should be taken?
- What will be the impact of that action?

This gap forms the core value proposition of **AERIS**. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

**Strengths**

**Limitations**
- No government workflow
- No decision intelligence
- No policy simulation

# AERIS Differentiation

AERIS is **not an AQI dashboard**.

It is an **AI-powered Urban Decision Intelligence Platform** that transforms environmental data into actionable recommendations for governments and smart city authorities.

# Why AERIS Wins

Unlike existing platforms that focus on monitoring and reporting, AERIS helps authorities understand **why pollution occurs, what actions should be taken, and what impact those actions are likely to have**. This bridges the gap between environmental monitoring and evidence-based decision-making. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
