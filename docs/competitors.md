# Market Gap

Current platforms answer:

"What is the air quality?"

AERIS answers:

- Why did pollution increase?
- What should the city do next?
- Which area needs immediate attention?
- Which intervention will have maximum impact?
- What happens if a specific policy is implemented?

---

# AERIS Differentiation

AERIS is not just an air quality dashboard.

It is an AI-powered Decision Intelligence Platform designed for city administrators and policy makers.

Core differentiators include:

- AI-powered recommendations
- Policy impact simulation
- Explainable AI
- Decision support dashboard
- Smart city focused workflows
- Action-oriented insights instead of passive monitoring

---

# Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

"Most air quality platforms tell cities what the AQI is.

AERIS tells cities why pollution is happening, what is likely to happen next, and what actions should be taken to improve air quality."
