# Project AERIS

# Stakeholders & User Personas

## Objective

Identify the primary stakeholders, end users, and their needs to ensure AERIS solves real-world environmental decision-making challenges.

---

# Primary Users (MVP)

| User | Goals | Challenges | AERIS Solution |
|------|-------|------------|----------------|
| City Administrators | Reduce pollution, improve city planning | Delayed decisions, fragmented data | AI Dashboard, Policy Simulation |
| Pollution Control Boards | Monitor & regulate pollution | Manual analysis, weak forecasting | AQI Forecasting, Root Cause Analysis |
| Municipal Operations Teams | Execute field actions | Slow hotspot identification | Hotspot Alerts, AI Recommendations |

---

# Secondary Users

| User | Purpose |
|------|---------|

| Health Departments | Health advisories & hospital preparedness |

# MVP Target Users

The hackathon prototype is designed primarily for:

- Municipal Corporations
- Pollution Control Boards
- Smart City Authorities
- City Administrators

Citizen-facing features are intentionally kept out of scope to maintain a focused MVP.

---

# Design Principles

- Government-first approach
- AI-assisted decision-making
- Explainable recommendations
- Simple and actionable interface
- Scalable for future citizen integration
